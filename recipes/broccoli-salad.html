Broccoli Salad

Description

A delicious creamy Broccoli Salad with whole grapes and sunflower seeds.

Ingredients:

  • 8 slices bacon
  • ⅓ cup sunflower seed kernels
  • 1 large head broccoli, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 cup seedless red grapes, halved
  • ⅓ cup diced red onion
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 2.5 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • ground black pepper to taste
  • Prep Time: 20 mins
  • Cook Time: 10 mins
  • Additional Time: 2 hrs
  • Total Time: 2 hrs 30 mins

Directions:

  1. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until evenly browned and crisp, about 10 minutes. Drain bacon slices on paper towels. Crumble 7 slices of bacon; mix with sunflower seeds in a resealable plastic bag and set aside.
  2. Combine broccoli, grapes, and onion in a large bowl.
  3. Whisk mayonnaise, vinegar, sugar, and pepper together in a separate bowl; crumble remaining slice of bacon and fold into dressing.
  4. Pour dressing over broccoli mixture; toss to coat evenly. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for flavors to blend, about 2 hours. Sprinkle bacon-sunflower seed mixture over salad before serving; mix well.
