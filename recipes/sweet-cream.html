Sweet Cream

Description

A sweet mascarpone-based cream that goes well with berries and can be thickened as a delicious cake frosting


Prep Time: 10

Cook Time: 10

Additional Time: 10

Total Time: 30 mins


Ingredients:

  • 200g heavy cream
  • 100g mascarpone
  • Vanilla
  • 40/50g powdered sugar

Directions:

  1. In a bowl, which together all ingredients but the mascarpone.
  2. Use a wooden spoon to incrementally fold in Mascarpone until fully incorporated
  3. Unclude berries of your choice or add to a cake for a delicious frosting!
