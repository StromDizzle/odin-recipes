Baked Salmon and Rice

Description

Salmon seasoned with salt, pepper, and garlic with basmati rice.

Ingredients:

  • 1-4 salmon filets
  • 1/2 cup of rice per salmon filet
  • Garlic or garlic paste
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1/2-3/4 stick of butter
  • Water
  • Prep Time: 5
  • Cook Time: 20
  • Total Time: 25 mins

Directions:

  1. In a pot, insert however much rice you plan to cook based on how many filets you are cooking. For example, if you are cooking 3 filets, you will need 1 1/2 cups of rice.
  2. Take the amount of rice you are cooking, double that amount, then add that much water to the pot. If you are cooking 1 1/2 cups of rice, you will add 3 cups of water.
  3. Add 1/4 stick of butter to the pot with the rice and water, add salt to taste.
  4. Bring pot to a rolling boil, then lower the heat so that the water is at a simmer, then start a timer for 10 minutes. It is important not to start the timer until you have brought the rice to a boil. That's it for the rice! Now on to the salmon!
  5. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees. Line a baking pan with aluminum foil, then place all salmon filets in the baking pan.
  6. Salt and pepper both sides of the salmon filets.
  7. In a small bowl, melt 1/4-1/2 stick of butter, then mix in garlic or garlic paste. I usually use about a tablespoon, but this is based on preference.
  8. Using a spoon, cover the salmon filets with the garlic butter mixture.
  9. Bake @ 400 for 20 minutes.
  10. Plate the salmon and rice and enjoy!
